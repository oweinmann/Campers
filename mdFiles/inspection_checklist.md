# Caravan Inspection Checklist

Use this checklist when visiting dealerships or inspecting caravans.

## Before the Visit

- ☐ Confirm appointment with dealer
- ☐ Bring measuring tape
- ☐ Bring camera for photos
- ☐ Bring notebook for notes
- ☐ Print this checklist
- ☐ Know your tow vehicle specifications (GVM, towing capacity, ball weight limit)

## External Inspection

### Structure and Build
- ☐ Confirm overall length is 15-18 feet
- ☐ Check roof is hard top (no canvas/hybrid sections)
- ☐ Inspect panel gaps and alignment
- ☐ Check for any damage or imperfections
- ☐ Verify chassis is galvanized or rust-protected
- ☐ Check ground clearance (higher is better for off-road)
- ☐ Inspect wheel wells for adequate clearance
- ☐ Verify quality of paint/finish

### Off-Road Features
- ☐ Check suspension type (independent preferred)
- ☐ Inspect shock absorbers
- ☐ Verify off-road tires (check tread, size)
- ☐ Check chassis protection (checkerplate, skid plates)
- ☐ Inspect coupling (DO35/DO45 for off-road)
- ☐ Verify stone guard on front
- ☐ Check wheel arch protection

### External Storage and Access
- ☐ Check external storage compartments (size, locks, seals)
- ☐ Verify gas bottle storage (capacity, accessibility)
- ☐ Check jerry can holders if needed
- ☐ Inspect door quality and seals
- ☐ Verify windows open smoothly and seal properly
- ☐ Check external shower (if included)
- ☐ Inspect awning (if included)

## Internal Inspection

### Layout and Space
- ☐ Confirm at least 2 bunk beds for children
- ☐ Measure bunk dimensions (length, width)
- ☐ Check bunk weight ratings
- ☐ Verify main bed size (Queen or larger preferred)
- ☐ Assess overall internal height (can adults stand comfortably?)
- ☐ Check walkway width
- ☐ Verify layout works for family of your size
- ☐ Test access to bunks (ladder, safety rails)

### Kitchen
- ☐ Check stove/cooktop (number of burners, type)
- ☐ Verify fridge size and type (3-way recommended)
- ☐ Check sink size and tap quality
- ☐ Assess storage space (cupboards, drawers)
- ☐ Check benchtop space and quality

### Bathroom
- ☐ Verify toilet type (cassette preferred for off-road)
- ☐ Check shower size and quality
- ☐ Inspect ventilation (fan, window)
- ☐ Check storage space
- ☐ Verify hot water system capacity

### Storage
- ☐ Check wardrobe/cupboard space
- ☐ Verify overhead locker quantity and size
- ☐ Check under-bed storage
- ☐ Inspect general storage solutions
- ☐ Check if storage is accessible while beds are made

### Build Quality
- ☐ Open and close all cupboards/drawers (smooth operation?)
- ☐ Check cabinet construction and materials
- ☐ Inspect upholstery quality
- ☐ Verify flooring type and quality
- ☐ Check wall construction and insulation
- ☐ Test all lights and switches
- ☐ Verify USB charging points
- ☐ Check 12V and 240V outlets (quantity, location)

## Technical Systems

### Electrical
- ☐ Confirm solar panel wattage (300W+ preferred)
- ☐ Verify battery capacity and type (AGM or Lithium)
- ☐ Check inverter capacity (if included)
- ☐ Verify battery management system
- ☐ Check electrical panel location and accessibility
- ☐ Test all internal lights
- ☐ Check external lights (running lights, brake lights, indicators)
- ☐ Verify mains hookup cable included

### Water System
- ☐ Confirm fresh water tank capacity
- ☐ Check grey water tank capacity
- ☐ Verify water pump quality
- ☐ Check water filter system (if included)
- ☐ Test all taps
- ☐ Check hot water system type and capacity
- ☐ Verify tank placement (protected from damage)
- ☐ Check tank level gauges work

### Gas System
- ☐ Confirm number of gas bottles
- ☐ Check gas bottle size/capacity
- ☐ Verify gas compliance certificate
- ☐ Check what runs on gas (stove, hot water, fridge)

### Climate Control
- ☐ Check ventilation (roof vents, windows, fans)
- ☐ Verify heating system (diesel, gas, electric)
- ☐ Check if air conditioning is included (optional)
- ☐ Test all vents open/close smoothly

## Documentation

### Check Provided Documents
- ☐ Owner's manual
- ☐ Warranty documentation
- ☐ Gas compliance certificate
- ☐ Electrical compliance certificate
- ☐ Weight specifications document
- ☐ Parts list
- ☐ Maintenance schedule
- ☐ Appliance manuals

### Dealer Information
- ☐ Get dealer contact information
- ☐ Confirm warranty terms and duration
- ☐ Verify what's included in warranty
- ☐ Ask about service center locations
- ☐ Confirm delivery timeframe
- ☐ Get written quote with all inclusions/extras
- ☐ Ask about trade-in options (if applicable)
- ☐ Inquire about financing options

## Test and Measurements

- ☐ Take photos of exterior (all angles)
- ☐ Take photos of interior (all areas)
- ☐ Take photos of specification plates
- ☐ Measure external length, width, height
- ☐ Measure internal dimensions
- ☐ Measure bunk bed dimensions
- ☐ Measure main bed dimensions
- ☐ Record Tare weight
- ☐ Record ATM
- ☐ Record ball weight
- ☐ Note model and serial number

## Questions for Dealer

- ☐ What is the current delivery time?
- ☐ Are there any current promotions?
- ☐ Can I customize the layout or options?
- ☐ What optional extras are available?
- ☐ Is a pre-delivery inspection included?
- ☐ Can I inspect before taking delivery?
- ☐ What is the payment schedule?
- ☐ Is delivery/transport included?
- ☐ Where is the closest service center?
- ☐ Are there any known issues with this model?
- ☐ Can I speak with current owners for reviews?
