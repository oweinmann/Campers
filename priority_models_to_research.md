## Models to Avoid

### Too Long
- Phoenix RV Off Road - 6.3m (21ft) - Exceeds 18ft max
- JB Caravans Scorpion - 6.4m (21ft) - Exceeds 18ft max
- Option RV Traction - 6.4m (21ft) - Exceeds 18ft max

### Hybrid Roof (Does Not Meet Hard Top Requirement)
- Track Trailer T4 Hybrid - Has hybrid roof sections
- Any "pop-top" models - Not hard top
- Most "Camper Trailers" - Often soft top or hybrid

### Price Concerns
- Premium brands over $100k:
  - Higher-end Jayco models (check each model)
  - Some premium Lotus models
  - Larger luxury off-road brands

### Important Considerations for Imported vs Australian-Made

**Australian-Made Advantages:**
- Better warranty support and service network
- Higher resale value typically
- Build quality often more consistent
- Parts availability easier
- Supporting local industry

**Imported Brand Advantages:**
- Significantly lower purchase price ($20k-40k savings)
- Modern features at lower price point
- More money for accessories/upgrades
- Less depreciation concern with lower initial cost

**Key Questions for Imported Brands:**
- What is the warranty period and what does it cover?
- Where are service centers located?
- How long have they been in Australia?
- Can I speak with existing owners?
- What is parts availability like?
- Who handles warranty claims?

## Dealer Contact Strategy

### Phone Script
"Hi, I'm looking for a new off-road caravan between 15 and 18 feet with at least 2 bunk beds for my children, a hard top roof, and a budget of around $100,000. Do you have any models that fit these requirements? I'd like to visit to see the specifications and layout."

### Email Template
```
Subject: Inquiry - Off-Road Caravan with Family Layout

Hi [Dealer Name],

I'm in the market for a new off-road caravan and would like to know if you have models that meet the following requirements:

- Length: 15-18 feet
- Layout: At least 2 bunk beds for children
- Roof: Hard top (not hybrid)
- Budget: Up to $100,000 AUD
- Off-road capable

Could you please provide:
1. Models that fit these requirements
2. Specification sheets or brochures
3. Current pricing and any promotions
4. Availability for inspection

I'm particularly interested in [specific model if known].

Thank you,
[Your name]
[Your contact details]
```
